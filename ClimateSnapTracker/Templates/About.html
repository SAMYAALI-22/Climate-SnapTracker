About Climate Snaptracker

Climate Snaptracker is an interactive platform that provides real-time climate and weather data from around the world. Our goal is to help users understand and track global climate patterns with ease.

“Explore the changing climate with just a click, from any corner of the globe.”

Using state-of-the-art technology, Climate Snaptracker visualizes weather patterns on a 3D globe, making it easy for you to dive into data and discover insights. Whether you're a researcher, student, or simply curious, our platform is designed for everyone.
